## Today I learned:
  1. Contrast is the difference between two colors and how good you can tell them appart.
  2. What is a good contrast? WCAG sais that a contrast of 4.5:1 is the absolute minimum for accesibility. A 7:1 contrast is recomended for best accessibility.
  3. But how to see the contrast or the level? There are special websites like WebAIM Contrast Checker, Color Contrast Checker, etc.
  4. If you make a color lighter it changes the contrast.

  5. For a good visual hierachie you can use headins and font sizes. A good visual hierachie is important to show the user imortant parts and have a clear layout.
  6. You can use callout boxes to drag attention to key concepts or buttons (CallToAction) like free trails.
