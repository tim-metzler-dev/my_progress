## Today I learned:
  1. left alignment is one of the most common ways to align text. It is easy to read and also to scan.
  2. right alignment is often used for secondary content and advirtisement.
  3. center alignment is often used for headings logos or elements/informations you want to bring attention to.
  4. Justified Alignment for Clean Text Blocks. This ensures that the text stratches between left and right margines.
  5. vertical alignment. It is used for name, email, and message fields. It is used to align elements around the vertical axis.

  6. Macro white space is the space between larger elements like images, text blocks, and buttons.
  7. Active white space is the space that is intentionally created to help guide the user's eye and draw attention to certain elements on the page.
  8. Passive white space is the space that is left when all the elements are on the site.
  9. Macro white space is the space between individual characters in a line of text.
