## Tosay I learned:
 1. I learned about margin and padding. Margins control the space outside an element and padding the space inside the element. They will be defined with px
      I. There are four margines:
        "margin-top"
        "margin-bottom"
        "margin-left"
        "margin-right"
         You can also only right down only margin and one/two/three/four numbers:
          margin: 10px;                  all the sites (margin types) will have a value of ten. all
          margin: 10px 20px;             This sets top and bottom to 10px and left and right to 20.   top&bottom, right&left
          margin: 10px 20px 30px;        This will set the top to ten, left and right to 20 and bottom to 30 pixel.   top, right&left, bottom
          margin: 10px 20px 30px 40px;   This will set the top to ten, right to 20, bottom to 30 pixel and left to 40px.   top, right, bottom, left
     II. So the padding element is the same, but with the difference, that it controls the space inside an element.
