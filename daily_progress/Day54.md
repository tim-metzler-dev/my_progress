## Today I learned:
1. There are three types of width and height.
     1. The normal type. This is the type that I'll use often in the next time. The other types will be important later.
        I will keep them in mind: width; height. We know them.
     2. The min type: min-width; min-height. These are the smallest possible apereance for the element. It don't goes smaller.
     3. The max type: max-width; max-height. These are the same as min but for the biggest possible appereance.
   But the last two aren't helpfull now. They will help at responsive web design.
